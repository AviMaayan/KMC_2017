Omics Mining for the Illuminating the Druggable Genome Project

Protein Similarity Matrices

'Dark' proteins were characterized using unsupervised clustering based on several bological datasets (e.g. GTEx), which enabled us to find associations between understudied and more well known proteins. Users can explore protein-protein associations based on gene expression and transcription using interactive similarity matrices.

Functional Predictions

Publically available RNA-seq data was used to infer Gene Ontology terms for 'dark' proteins. Predictions correctly return known ontology terms as well predict new biological functions for 'dark' proteins.

TCGA Analysis

Gene expression and patient survival data from the TCGA were used to investigate several 'dark' kinases identified as part of a cluster of mitosis related kianses that were co-expressed in CCLE cancer cell lines. The 'dark' kinase PKMYT1 is negatively associated with patient survival.

Jupyter Notebooks

Jupyter notebooks were used to explore kinase, ion channel, and G-protein coupled receptor clusters identified based on co-expression in the CCLE. Common biological themes were identified and biological function was inferred for 'dark' proteins.
